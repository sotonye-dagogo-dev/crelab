This Privacy Policy explains how {platformConfig.name} ('we', 'us', 'our') collects, uses, and protects your personal data under the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) 2023.

1. Data Controller

{platformConfig.name} is the data controller for personal data collected through the platform. Registered address: Lagos, Nigeria. Contact: privacy@crelab.ng.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Account data — email, phone number, full name, password (hashed)
  • Profile data — bio, location, years active, experience level, niche tags, portfolio items
  • Transaction data — bookings, payments, escrow records, dispute history
  • Usage data — analytics, page views, interactions, device information, log data
  • Consent records — NDPR consent timestamps, scope, withdrawal records

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Consent — you explicitly opt in for marketing, analytics, and profile visibility
  • Contract performance — processing necessary to facilitate bookings, payments, and escrow
  • Legitimate interest — fraud prevention, platform security, service improvement
  • Legal obligation — records retention, regulatory compliance, dispute resolution

4. Data Sharing

We share your data only as necessary to provide the platform services:

  • Providers / Clients — booking-related information shared between parties in context of a booking
  • Paystack — payment processing (card data never touches Crelab servers)
  • Cloudinary / Mux — media hosting, transcoding, and delivery
  • Google Drive API — portfolio sync (public folders only, no OAuth scopes)
  • Sanity — blog content management
  • Resend — transactional email delivery
  • Authorities — when required by law or valid legal process

We never sell your personal data to third parties.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary:

  • Account data — while your account is active, plus 2 years after deletion for audit purposes
  • Financial data — 7 years (statutory audit requirement)
  • Analytics data — 26 months
  • Consent records — permanent record of consent and withdrawal

6. Your Rights Under NDPR

You have the following rights regarding your personal data. To exercise them, visit your account settings or contact us.

  • Right of Access — request a copy of your data via /api/account/export
  • Right to Rectification — correct inaccurate data in your profile settings
  • Right to Erasure — request account deletion via /api/account/delete
  • Right to Data Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to Restrict Processing — limit how we use your data under certain circumstances
  • Right to Object — object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Right to Withdraw Consent — withdraw consent at any time via /api/account/consent

7. International Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside Nigeria. When we transfer data internationally, we ensure adequate safeguards are in place through Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or Binding Corporate Rules (BCRs).

8. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3)
  • Row-Level Security (RLS) on all user data queries
  • Strict access controls — no direct database access outside the API layer
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing

9. Cookies

We use essential and analytics cookies. You can manage your preferences in the Cookie Policy section. Essential cookies operate on a legitimate interest basis; analytics cookies require your consent.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be notified via email and in-app notification. Continued use after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@crelab.ng. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Nigeria Data Protection Commission (NDPC).
